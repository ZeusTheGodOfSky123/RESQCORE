About Us

ResQcore is a pioneering startup developing self-heating, flame-free life jackets to prevent hypothermia and save lives in extreme environments. Our jackets are engineered for rapid deployment in emergencies where warmth and time are critical.

We're revolutionizing cold-weather safety gear for military, maritime, trekking, disaster relief, and civilians in extreme climates.

Learn About the Tech

Our Technology

Our jackets use a water-activated chemical heating system that is safe, flame-free, and battery-free. Lightweight and compact, ResQcore jackets provide 6 to 8 hours of sustained warmth.

  • Instant warmth through exothermic reaction
  • No electricity or flame
  • Durable, reusable, field-tested materials
